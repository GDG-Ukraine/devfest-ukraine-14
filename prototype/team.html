About us

Google knows throughout the earth. Even people who fly out of the planet knows about Google. Google intimately penetrated into human ordinariness. Google's services have become synonymous with simple things online. For ordinary users, all these technologies look beautiful web services, and in fact, each of these services is a huge stack of technologies. Number of technological innovations and IT industries in which Google is creating new products is enormous. Get to know them all is difficult, but realistic. This is why people have started to associate in small local groups and share experiences. Subsequently, the following groups of people started calling Google Developer Group. And day by day, event by event, they became more and more. The first in Ukraine were GDG Kyiv-Center, the guys are so carried away that were the first in the gtug ranking in the world. One of their event - Global Android Dev Camp. This hakaton gathered a lot of teams. It was at the event in a wonderful atmosphere of fun, conceived the idea to organize a GDG in Lviv. Lviv - the ancient city with many IT people; and people who use both Google, everywhere a lot. So, coming back from android-hakathon, the train Lviv-Kyiv, decided that this should organize Lviv GDG!
